F1: Apex is optimized to run on a wide range of hardware configurations.

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit / macOS 11 / Ubuntu 20.04
  • Processor: Intel i5-6600K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1060 / AMD RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 15 GB available space
  • Additional: Broadband internet connection for multiplayer

Recommended Requirements

  • OS: Windows 11 64-bit / macOS 13 / Ubuntu 22.04
  • Processor: Intel i7-9700K / AMD Ryzen 7 3700X

  • Graphics: NVIDIA RTX 3070 / AMD RX 6800

  • Storage: 15 GB available space (SSD recommended)
  • Additional: Racing wheel and pedals for optimal experience

  • Racing Wheels: Logitech G29/G920/G923, Thrustmaster T150/T300/T500, Fanatec CSL/ClubSport
  • Controllers: Xbox Series X|S, PlayStation 5 DualSense, Steam Controller
